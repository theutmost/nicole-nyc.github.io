Redesign of Connectected Vehicle Mobile Applications

A complete redesign of client's current mobile app, focusing on latest design style trends and the better user experience for Hyundai Blue Link, KIA UVO, and Genesis Connected Service‚Äôs users

Project Overview

In Station Digital Media, I worked closely with Hyundai Korea to redesign with new look and feel of Blue Link mobile applications. Hyundai Blue Link, KIA UVO, Genesis Connected Service are connected vehicle system using an embedded telematics to gather vehicle Car Status information. The system utilizes mobile apps to allow the customer to Remote Engine Start/Stop, Charge Start/Stop, Climate Start/Stop, Lock or Unlock their vehicle.

Copy and Language

As a native Korean speaker, I was able to translate and localize English content in the target language (Korean) for the in-app pop-up messages and front UI copies.

Sketching and Brainstorming
Sketching and organizing with the high level UI element print-outs helps to brainstorm, communicate layout and hierarchy until designing high-fidelity screens.

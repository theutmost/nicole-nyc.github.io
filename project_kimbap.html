The Kim Bap Branding

The Kim Bap Restaurant offers fusion Korean dish with unique retro theme grand-opened in the fall 2016.

I was commissioned to create a fresh, authentic design from concept to completion for the brand identity and interior. Influenced by cultural tradition and Korean retro style in 1970-80's, with a goal to offer a friendly visual experience to 1st generation people in K-town.

The Kim Bap Logo Design with some concept options

Main Wall Menu Hierarchy of languages, menu list, and other contents are carefully considered by discussing with the business owner and listening to their goal.

Table Menu I worked closely with a product photographer to decide the style and its composition for the menu designs. While designing menus, some original images with the white background needed to be edited (lights, shadows, and shapes) using photoshop.
